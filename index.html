Team Background

Building dependable testing methods for modern software systems.

The group is rooted in software engineering research at Nanjing University and works at the intersection of software testing, computer vision, empirical software engineering, and human-centered quality assurance. Our recent work emphasizes practical testing automation for mobile apps, especially when graphical interfaces, cross-platform behaviors, and natural-language task descriptions are central to the problem.

Over the past several years, the team has published in leading venues including ICSE, FSE, TSE, TOSEM, ASE, and CSUR, with a consistent focus on methods that can transfer from lab settings into real engineering workflows and software testing education.

Mission

Bridge advanced testing research with deployable engineering practice.

The group builds methods that work on realistic mobile apps rather than idealized benchmarks alone.

Method Stack

Vision, program analysis, reinforcement learning, and LLM reasoning.

Research combines GUI perception, scenario understanding, and automation pipelines for end-to-end quality assurance.

Output

Publications, datasets, tools, and teaching-facing testing workflows.

The work is designed to support both academic impact and student training in software engineering.

Research Areas

Four connected themes shape the group's work.

01

Mobile GUI Testing

Visual understanding, layout analysis, and scenario-guided exploration for testing real mobile interfaces across platforms and devices.

02

LLMs for Software Testing

Test script generation, migration, retrieval-augmented reasoning, and automated support for testing workflows using large language models.

03

Crowdsourced Testing Intelligence

Prioritization, clustering, report assessment, and consistency detection through joint image-text analysis and human-centered modeling.

04

Testing Education and Tooling

Reproducible datasets, benchmark design, hands-on testing platforms, and course-facing teaching material for modern software quality engineering.
